More ways to save Water

We must save water for our future, if water not conserved we will face scarcity of water, water is used to plant trees, if plants wouldn't survive we will not able to survive, below are some ways to save water:-

Bawris

 With time the bawris fell into disuse and garbage started piling in these reservoirs. However, becuse of the acute water shrotage, people in these areas have had to rethink. The bawris are being revived. Today the situation is that inspite of scanty rains these places are managing their water needs well.

 Drip Irrigation

Drip Irrigation is a technique of watering plants by making use of narrow tubings which deliver water directly at the base of the plant.
