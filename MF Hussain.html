M.F.Hussain
 Maqbool Fida Husain was an Indian artist known for executing bold, vibrantly coloured narrative paintings in a
modified Cubist style. He was one of the most celebrated and internationally
recognised Indian artists of the 20th century. He was one of the founding members of Bombay Progressive Artists' Group. M.F.On Madhuri Dixit's 49th birthday.
